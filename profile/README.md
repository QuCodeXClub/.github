## Code. Create. Innovate.

CODEX Club is a technology community built for developers, creators, and problem solvers passionate about programming, software development, and emerging technologies.

We encourage collaborative learning, practical development, and innovation through projects, technical sessions, coding challenges, and community-driven initiatives.

---

## Focus Areas

**Software Engineering**  
Building strong foundations in programming, development practices, and software solutions.

**Problem Solving & Algorithms**  
Strengthening logical thinking through algorithmic challenges and competitive programming.

**Web & Application Development**  
Creating modern web and mobile applications using current technologies.

**Artificial Intelligence & Emerging Technologies**  
Exploring AI, machine learning, and next-generation technology trends.

**Innovation & Projects**  
Collaborating on real-world projects, hackathons, and technology solutions.

---

## Community Goals

- Promote continuous learning and technical growth
- Encourage open collaboration and knowledge sharing
- Build practical solutions through technology
- Support developers at every stage of their journey
